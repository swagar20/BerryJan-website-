Welcome to Berryjan

About Berryjan

Berryjan is a premium beauty brand founded by Jane Swagar, an experienced professional with a vision to bring world-class Korean skincare to the Philippines. We specialize in rebranding high-quality OEM products to match local skin types and needs.

Our mission is to make luxury skincare accessible, affordable, and inclusive — with a focus on innovation, gender-specific formulations, and sun-safe routines.
