About Berryjan

Berryjan is a proudly Philippine-based skincare brand that offers high-quality, rebranded Korean skincare products. We partner with certified Korean OEM/ODM manufacturers to develop skin-friendly, climate-adapted, and result-driven products for both men and women.

Our mission is to deliver effective skincare with authenticity, innovation, and affordability at its core. Whether you're battling oily skin, pigmentation, dryness, or sun damage – Berryjan has a solution made just for you.
